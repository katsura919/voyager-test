# Spain Digital Nomad Playbook
*Source: by Abie Maxey (Coda)*

## What’s this visa about and do I qualify?

### What’s Spain’s Digital Nomad Visa?
Spain’s Digital Nomad Visa (officially “Visado de Teletrabajador”) allows non-EU remote workers to live legally in Spain while working for companies or clients outside Spain. It’s one of Europe’s most attractive options for digital nomads. Introduced in 2023 as part of Spain’s Startup Act, the visa lets non-EU citizens (including UK nationals) live & work remotely in Spain for up to five years.

---

### Who is eligible?

#### Basic Qualifications:
- **Age:** 18+
- **Nationality:** Non-EU/EEA citizens only
- **Work Status:** Remote employee OR freelancer/entrepreneur
- **Professional credentials:** Either:
  - A university degree (bachelor’s or higher), OR
  - 3+ years documented work experience in your field

#### Income Requirements (2026)
- **Single applicant:** €2,894/month minimum (gross, before taxes)
- **With spouse/partner:** €4,017/month minimum
- **Each additional dependent:** Add 25% of base amount

> 📝 **Note:** These thresholds are tied to Spain’s minimum wage and can increase over time.

#### Other Requirements
- Clean criminal record (past 5 years)
- Valid passport with at least 12+ months remaining
- Private health insurance valid in Spain
- Applicant must not have been a resident in Spain in the past 5 years

---

### Work Requirements

**For employees:**
- You must have an employment contract with a company outside Spain
- Company must have been operating for 1+ year
- You need a letter from your employer confirming remote work permission

**For freelancers:**
- At least 80% of your income must come from clients outside Spain
- You can work up to 20% for Spanish clients
- Your business must be established for 1+ year
